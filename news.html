Stewardship Week of Prayer

You are welcome to this revival week. Summary of stewardship revival week message, God First, with sis Grace Otieno Day One

1. Irrefutable principles of stewardship:

i) Ownership- God;

ii) Responsibility- given to man;

iii) Accountability - expected of man

iv) Reward- by God to man.

2. Areas to put God First: time; temple (body); talent and treasure.

3. Good management of time, temple, applying talent gives treasure out of which tithe is returned to God and in appreciation of abundance, offering is given.

4. God is first, its man who fails to recognise and acknowledge this. As a result, he/ she fears and worries about:

i) basic needs;

ii) personal worth/ value;

iii) things beyond his/her control.

5. Purpose to put God First in all spheres of life, He is mightily able; He will provide the basic needs; He will lift up and give value, and take care of tomorrow.Put God First and taste how sweet God is.
